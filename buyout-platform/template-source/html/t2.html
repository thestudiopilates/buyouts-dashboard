Studio Buyouts are private Pilates classes only. We're unable to accommodate food, beverages, or full event setups inside the studio. This policy protects our equipment and ensures the space is ready for classes before and after your event.

That said, we love helping you plan a great overall experience. Here are some nearby spots we recommend:

If You're at Our West Midtown Location
The Grove - a short stroll down Trabert to where it meets the Beltline, less than a quarter mile away. Great outdoor seating, food, and drinks on weekends.

If You're at Our Emory Location
Double Zero - right next door. They offer several private-ish seating areas and often do not require a buyout if you contact them in advance.
